Courses

The courses available for children as well as adults. The duration for the award of certificate is 3 years for adults and 5 years for children.

Drawing & Sketching

Learn the fundamentals of composition and drawing including charcoal, pencil, and ink techniques.

Water color painting

Explore the beauty of watercolor in this class for all experience levels.

Acrylic Painting

Acrylic painting, an art world full of versatility and bright, bold colors. Acrylics are water based, it offers opacity that makes this medium especially appealing to beginners.

Oil on Canvas Painting

Oil painting is the process of painting with pigments with a medium of drying oil as the binder.

Glass painting

Glass painting is one of the most attractive art form. Glass painting gives the presence of a fairy-tale.Glass art amplifies its beauty when the light passes through it.

Terracota

Painting terracotta pots is a fun way to add color to your home or garden.Put your personal touch on your container display. Learn how to paint your own clay pots .

Tanjore painting

Tanjore Painting is one of the most popular forms of classical South Indian painting. It is the native art form of Thanjavur city of Tamil Nadu.

Clay modelling

Learn how to do clay moulding and make exciting clay toys at Hobby Ideas. Explore wide variety of clay modelling art and craft ideas for kids and adults.

Quilling(paper filigree)

Quilling or paper filigree is an art form that involves the use of strips of paper that are rolled, shaped, and glued together to create decorative designs.

Diploma Certificate for kids and adults

We provide diploma certificate for anyone who finishes the course. Teacher training available too!
